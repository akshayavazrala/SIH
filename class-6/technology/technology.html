Technology

Explore the world of technology through interactive lessons, coding activities, and digital projects designed for Class 6 students

Why Technology is Important for Class 6 Students

Technology education at the Class 6 level introduces students to the digital world and helps them develop essential skills for the 21st century. It fosters creativity, problem-solving abilities, and logical thinking.

Through hands-on activities and interactive learning, students learn how to use technology responsibly, create digital content, and understand the basics of how technology works.

Our curriculum is designed specifically for Odisha students, incorporating local examples and contexts to make learning more relevant and engaging.

Develops Computational Thinking

Encourages logical reasoning and problem-solving approaches

Fosters Creativity

Allows students to create digital content and express ideas

Digital Literacy

Prepares students for a technology-driven world

Collaboration Skills

Teaches how to work together on digital projects

What You'll Learn in Class 6 Technology

Computer Basics

  • Parts of a computer and their functions
  • Input and output devices
  • Operating systems and software
  • Basic computer operations

Typing & Word Processing

  • Proper typing techniques
  • Creating and formatting documents
  • Adding images and tables
  • Saving and printing documents

Digital Art & Design

  • Basic drawing tools
  • Creating simple digital artwork
  • Understanding colors and shapes
  • Introduction to graphic design

Introduction to Coding

  • What is coding and why it matters
  • Basic programming concepts
  • Visual programming with blocks
  • Creating simple animations

Internet Safety

  • Safe browsing practices
  • Protecting personal information
  • Identifying online risks
  • Digital citizenship

Technology in Daily Life

  • How technology helps in education
  • Technology in communication
  • Smart devices and their uses
  • Future technology trends
