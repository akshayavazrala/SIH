Preview - Chapter Summary
Characteristics of Living Things:
  • Need food, water, and air
  • Grow and develop
  • Reproduce
  • Respond to stimuli
  • Move
  • Excrete waste
Key Concepts:
  • All living things have basic life processes
  • Different organisms have different habitats
  • Body structure is related to function
  • Plants and animals are both living things
