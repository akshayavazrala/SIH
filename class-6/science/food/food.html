Preview - Chapter Summary
Food Sources:
  • Plants: Fruits, vegetables, grains, pulses
  • Animals: Milk, eggs, meat, fish
Key Concepts:
  • All food comes from plants or animals
  • Plants prepare their own food through photosynthesis
  • Animals depend on plants or other animals for food
  • Food provides energy and helps in growth
