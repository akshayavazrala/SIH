How to Play the Food & Vitamin Memory Game

Welcome to the Food & Vitamin Memory Game! Here's how to play:

  • Click on cards to flip them and reveal foods or vitamins.
  • Match foods with their corresponding vitamins to earn points.
  • Each correct match reveals a fun fact about the food and its nutritional benefits.
  • You have 7 minutes to complete each level.
  • Earn bonus points for completing levels quickly.
  • Complete all levels to earn the Nutrition Expert badge!

Good luck and have fun learning about food and nutrition!
